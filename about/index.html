A Brief History

ZooMania

ZooMania was created by Koala Komander somewhere around the year 2005. ZooMania had two iterations. In her second iteration of ZooMania, KK brought on Animalover44, Catc, and Penguinman to the administrative team.

Toward the end of its life, ZooMania partnered up with the Zoo Tycoon 2 Design Centre and would ‘cross-host’ Zoo Tycoon mods, hence why you might see a ZT2DC file on occasion.

Northern Skies

Around the year 2008 or 2009, Catc was invited by Simba and Otter Lord of the Aurora Designs team to help them start a new forum they were calling ‘Northern Skies’. Northern Skies grew to become the largest Zoo Tycoon community of its time and was active for a couple of years.

Artifex

Artifex was originally the official Zoo Tycoon 2 design team at Zoo Admin but took on a life of its own as a passion project between some of the best designers in the community at the time. The original Zoo Tycoon 2 user-made expansion team, they are known for creating Arabian Nights and Island Excursions.

Where does ZooBerry come in?

Since none of us had any money back then, all mods were hosted on free file hosting services like SaveFile or MediaFire. Luckily, most files hosted on MediaFire have survived the test of time (some files are as old as 16 years!). Files hosted on SaveFile, not so much.

These forums and websites are no longer around. Zoomania was never transferred to Tapatalk before Invisionfree and Zetaboards shut down, so the only archive available is on archive.org. Northern Skies, luckily, was transferred and saved (or at least a version of it), so most of its content is still accessible.

The purpose behind this website is to conserve these downloads before more are lost–specifically files hosted by websites I once had a hand in running, or as a member of its design team. At the moment, this website is a trial run. Hosting files does cost money, so it’s all dependant on how much I’m able to handle. At the moment, I do not have a donation setup–but I might put one up in the future if people are keen on supporting this effort. :)

Who are you?

My handle these days is Goosifer. Back in the day I used to run Zoomania and Northern Skies, and was also a freshman member of the Artifex modding team. You may try your best to guess which one, I wasn’t very prolific :P. However, it might be obvious after reading the previous section what I used to go by.

I was only a kid when I helped run these websites (or in Artifex’s case, made mods for people to download). I didn’t quite have any sysadmin experience let alone a job that allowed me to keep them going. I still tried, but I was quite young and so made many mistakes.

Where did all of the other admins go?

As all things go on the internet, people move on and stop signing in. We were all in high school at the time so you can say we all grew up and developed other interests over time as we got older. :) Some of us moved on to become lawyers, others became doctors, some animal rehab coordinators, and on and on!

Why now?

These days I am a computer science major and know a lot more about web development, programming, and the reality of hosting your own website than I did way back when. It helps that this doubles as a project I can share with others to show what I can do ;)

Will you backup legacy mods from other forums not from ZooMania, Northern Skies, or Artifex?

I need to see what my baseline hosting costs will be after a few months of running ZooBerry. If all looks good and reasonable, I might consider backing up other forums as long as I have permission from an admin in charge. I would also need them to express interest first. For the moment, I will have to respectfully decline until I can see where things go. That said, if you’re interested, feel free to reach out to me on Discord or at zooberry@goosifer.io and I can keep you posted if things change!

What about new mods released today?

If you’re a designer and wish to host your new mod on ZooBerry today, I would be happy to do so. This is agreeable versus full website backups since I can say I’m not committing to 300 files at a time. Please check out the contact page for more information.

Mod ownership

ZooBerry believes in mod ownership and therefore if you are author to a mod hosted here on ZooBerry and wish to have it taken down, please do not hesitate to message me at zooberry@goosifer.io and I can take it down.

Future mods

Since ZooBerry is a statically-generated website, this means we do not have dynamic functionality in a backend somewhere that lets us send emails out. You can expect any new mods added to the ZooBerry database to be advertised at any of the links in the links page. You may also follow ZooBerry on Mastadon
