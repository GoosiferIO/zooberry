    Description

    Thanks for downloading Unlimited Feather Overload (U.F.O.)!

    A new expansion pack full of prehistoric fluffy pals!!

    ZooTekPhoenix 20th Anniversary promo

    IMPORTANT NOTE!!!

    Cuban Giant Owl uses Borsato’s ui99ztd + LANG–ZBorsato.dll content! (See below at #dependencies)

    However, it will work correctly even if you don’t use these patches.

    Compatibility Guide

    • The whole pack is compatible with CC.

    • Excluding Baptornis and Pelagornis, all other singles should be compatible with DD.

    • Nyctosaurus is compatible with Borsato’s Pteranodon & Hawkkeye’s Ichthyornis & Hesperornis.

    • Nyctosaurus is a non-tankable animal. In these mixed exhibits, use water terrain instead!

    • Quetzalcoatlus is compatible with Borsato’s Torosaurus & Hawkkeye’s Parksosaurus.

    • Dromaeosaurus & Gorgosaurus are compatible with BlueFang’s Lambeosaurus (vanilla), Borsato’s Parasaurolophus & Hawkkeye’s Nasutoceratops, Pentaceratops & Panoplosaurus.

    • Argentavis is compatible with Borsato’s Thylacosmilus.

    • Baptornis is compatible with Borsato’s Tylosaurus.

    • Titanis is compatible with HENDRIX’s American Mastodon.

    • Yutyrannus is compatible with BlueFang’s Caudipteryx (vanilla), Borsato’s Protarchaeopteryx & HENDRIX’s Incisivosaurus.

    • Albertosaurus is compatible with BlueFang’s Triceratops (vanilla) & Hawkkeye’s Edmontosaurus & Edmontonia.

    • Daspletosaurus is compatible with Borsato’s Corythosaurus & Hawkkeye’s Centrosaurus, Chasmosaurus, Chirostenotes & Euoplocephalus.

    • Nanuqsaurus is compatible with Hawkkeye’s Pachyrhinosaurus.

    • Tarbosaurus is compatible with Hawkkeye’s Mononykus, Saurolophus, Tarchia & Therizinosaurus.

    • Guanlong is compatible with Hawkkeye’s Chungkingosaurus, Gigantspinosaurus & Mamenchisaurus.
