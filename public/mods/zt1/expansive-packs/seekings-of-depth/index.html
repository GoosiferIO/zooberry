Content List

Animals

  • Cladoselache. Devonian, North America.
  • Orthacanthus. Devonian, North America.
  • Stethacanthus. Devonian, Eurasia.
  • Edestus. Carboniferous, Eurasia.
  • Xenacanthus. Carboniferous, Eurasia.
  • Helicoprion. Triassic, Many Oceans Worldwide.
  • Hybodus. Cretaceous, Many Oceans Worldwide.
  • Ptychodus. Cretaceous, Many Oceans Worldwide.
  • Ginsu Shark. Cretaceous, Many Oceans Worldwide.
  • Schizorhiza. Cretaceous, Africa.
  • Onchopristis. Cretaceous, Africa.
  • Megalodon. Ice Age, Many Oceans Worldwide.

Bonus items

  • Xiphactinus. Cretaceous, North America. This one is made to be a voracious eater! Take good care of them!
  • Megalodon Jaws. Neutral exhibit & water placeable!
  • Fossil Display. Neutral exhibit & water placeable!
  • Ammonite Display. Scenery item.

Compatibility Guide

  • Compatible with ZT CC

Panthalassa Bay Cladoselache + Orthacanthus + Stethacanthus + Khaydar’s Dunkleosteus

Tethys’ Teeth Edestus + Xenacanthus + Helicoprion

Jurassic Tank Xiphactinus + Hybodus + Ptychodus + Ginsu Shark

African Ancient Coast Onchopristis + Schizorhiza

Deep Sea Kings Megalodon + Khaydar’s Basilosaurus + Khaydar’s Dorudon
