    Update August 23, 2023

    Published by Goosifer on 23 Aug 2023

    Update log for ZooBerry on August 23, 2023.

    Concerning ZooBerry

    There is currently a bug I am trying to work out where lists aren’t completely sorted in order across all pagination. I can’t give an estimate when that will be fixed but hopefully soon!

    I am also looking into the issue where the authors list is trying to embed a thumbnail next to an author’s name in error.
